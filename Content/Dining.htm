Dining in Peachtree

Don't feel like cooking tonight? With the selection of dining options that Peachtree City offers, you will always have quick access to top-tier restaurants.

Check out the few that we listed below.

The Beirut

The Beirut

Ranked as the Number One ranked restaurant on TripAdvisor; the Beirut, a Lebanese Restaurant, without a doubt deserves its due praise. The Beirut offers a variety of Mediterranean, Lebanese, and Middle Eastern dishes. This is a great spot for both lunch and dinner options. The experience is unforgettable, so make sure to check out this spot if you're in town!

Grazing Here

Grazing here

The Grazing Here is a barn-inspired eatery & bar that serves burgers from grass-fed beef. Customers are able to order from a menu or decide to create their own burgerpiece (burger + masterpiece). If that isn’t enough, the Grazing Here regularly features live music regularly.

The Georgia Shrimp Company

The Georgia Shrimp company

The Georgia Shrimp Company is known for its southern-tinged seafood dishes served in a low-key space. This is the best option when considering diving into the seafood scene in Peachtree City. With live music and a bar with a tapas menu, The Georgia Shrimp Company sure is a catch!
